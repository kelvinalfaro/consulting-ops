Users must review the authoritative solicitation and amendments, confirm eligibility and deadlines, obtain appropriate professional advice, approve pricing and representations, sign required documents, and perform final submission themselves.

Use of this software does not create an attorney-client, consultant-client, fiduciary, or procurement relationship and does not guarantee compliance, responsiveness, selection, or award.
