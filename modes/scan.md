# Opportunity scan

Read `config/rfp_sources.yml` and the firm profile. Search enabled feeds/APIs and, when the AI has web access, run configured search terms across official procurement portals, nonprofit/foundation notices, higher education, and public-sector sources. Preserve URLs and issuer/title/deadline evidence in `data/rfp_pipeline.md`. Put only concrete, domain-relevant solicitations under `Pending`; retain useful portal/directory discoveries under `Source leads`; reject definitions, templates, guides, and other informational articles. Deduplicate against every pipeline section, tracker, and normalized records. Report opportunities, source leads, rejections, and provider errors separately. Do not bypass access controls.
