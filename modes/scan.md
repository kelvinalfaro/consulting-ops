# Opportunity scan

Read `config/rfp_sources.yml` and the firm profile. Search enabled feeds/APIs and, when the AI has web access, run configured search terms across official procurement portals, nonprofit/foundation notices, higher education, and public-sector sources. Preserve URLs and issuer/title/deadline evidence in `data/rfp_pipeline.md`. Put only concrete, domain-relevant solicitations under `Pending`; retain useful portal/directory discoveries under `Source leads`; reject definitions, templates, guides, and other informational articles. Deduplicate canonical URLs and issuer/title/deadline fingerprints across every pipeline section, tracker, and normalized record. Report opportunities, source leads, rejection-reason counts, provider errors, and persistent source-health failures separately. Use `--dry-run` before a live scan when validating configuration. Do not bypass access controls.
