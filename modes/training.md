# Training or certification assessment

Use `npx consulting-ops training <course-or-credential>` to create the evidence worksheet. Assess strategic fit, credible client demand, capability gaps, signaling value, time and cost, alternatives, and the smallest bounded validation test. Use current course facts supplied by the user or verified from an authoritative source. Recommend Invest, Test, Defer, or Decline only after evidence review; otherwise retain Needs evidence.
