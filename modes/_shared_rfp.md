# Shared RFP rules

Read `AGENTS.md`, `capability_statement.md`, `config/company_profile.yml`, `modes/_company_profile.md`, the normalized opportunity, and the authoritative solicitation before evaluating or drafting.

## Evidence rules

- Firm claims require an approved user-layer source.
- RFP requirements require a source page, section, URL, or amendment reference.
- Unknown facts remain unknown and become clarification questions.
- Never imply a certification, partner commitment, reference, capacity, price, or result that has not been confirmed.
- Preserve conflicting requirements and flag them; do not silently reconcile them.

## Evaluation order

1. Confirm solicitation identity, source, version, amendments, and dates.
2. Extract submission instructions, mandatory requirements, attachments, page limits, and evaluation criteria.
3. Apply hard gates: deadline, eligibility, capacity, terms, and professional scope.
4. Score capability fit, mission fit, differentiation, feasibility, commercial attractiveness, win conditions, and strategic value.
5. State confidence and evidence gaps.
6. Recommend `Bid`, `Conditional Bid`, `No Bid`, or `Needs Clarification`.

A failed hard gate defaults to `No Bid`. An unknown hard gate defaults to `Needs Clarification`. A person may override the recommendation, but the report must retain the original recommendation and document the rationale.

## Drafting order

1. Compliance matrix
2. Clarification questions
3. Response outline mapped to evaluation criteria
4. Technical approach and work plan
5. Team and past performance
6. Pricing assumptions and required forms
7. Executive summary and cover letter
8. Final compliance and visual review

The system never performs final submission.
