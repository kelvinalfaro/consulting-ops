# Jurisdiction and procurement-market calibration

This is the consulting-native equivalent of a regional career calibration. Use `node consulting-ops.mjs jurisdiction <jurisdiction-or-market>` to create the assessment. Verify vendor registration, entity and tax requirements, certifications, insurance, data/privacy rules, governing law, language, currency, payment terms, local-presence or teaming requirements, accessibility, and submission-platform constraints from authoritative sources. Separate hard eligibility gates from preferences and weighted fit. Do not claim eligibility until evidence is recorded.
