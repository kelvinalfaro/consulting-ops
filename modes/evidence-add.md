# Add firm evidence

This is the consulting-native equivalent of adding a role, project, or publication to a career profile. Stage the source with `npx consulting-ops evidence-add <URL-or-file-or-description>`. Verify authorship, the firm's actual role, dates, outcomes, metrics, confidentiality, client-name permission, and intended evidence type. Show the exact proposed diff to the approved destination (`case-studies/`, `team/`, `writing-samples/`, or `capability_statement.md`) and wait for explicit confirmation before writing. Staged intake files are not approved evidence.
