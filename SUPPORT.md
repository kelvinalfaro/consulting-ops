# Support

For setup and workflow questions, open a GitHub discussion or issue with the `consulting-ops --help`, `consulting-ops doctor`, and relevant error output. Remove client names, proprietary solicitation content, credentials, and firm-confidential evidence before posting.

Use the private reporting route in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) for vulnerabilities. The project cannot provide legal advice, procurement authority, or guarantees about eligibility, compliance, or award outcomes.
