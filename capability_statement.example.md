# Example Consulting LLC capability statement

## Company profile

Provide the legal business name, location, contact information, website, and only the certifications or classifications the firm actually holds.

## Core competencies

- Service area: concise description of what the firm delivers and for whom.

## Differentiators

- A specific, supportable reason a client would select this firm.

## Past performance

### Example engagement

- Client: use an approved name or an accurate anonymized description.
- Challenge: what the client needed.
- Approach: what the firm actually did.
- Results: verified outcomes and metrics only.
- Reference availability: yes, no, or by request.

## Key personnel

List approved bios, roles, credentials, and availability. Do not imply that occasional collaborators are committed to an opportunity without confirming them.
